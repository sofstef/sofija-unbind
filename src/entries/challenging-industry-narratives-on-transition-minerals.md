I was invited to speak at the London Mining Network's and Future Narratives Lab launch event of their report into challenging industry narratives on transition minerals. This work was prompted by the desire to equip campaigners and communities with effective counter-narratives in the context of powerful narratives of urgency deployed by industry and governments.
